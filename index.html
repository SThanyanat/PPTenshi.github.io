Welcome to Amane Kanata Fan Website

The idea of this site was born from the sudden announcement of her graduation from Hololive on Dec 27th, 2025, which marks her 6th anniversary. This was announced on December 2nd, 2025, surprising the whole VTuber industry. In other words, Kanata will be quitting her career as a VTuber and will completely disappear from the entertainment industry.

It is truly unfortunate that she had chosen to give up her path as a streamer/idol, given how much she loves streaming and singing. The decision was due to her being overloaded with work while having to deal with the worsening effect of Meniere's disease, which is causing hearing loss. As of now, she is deaf in her right ear.

As one of her fans since 2021, I am hoping to share her stories and achievements up until now because she is a very kind and determined angel who has never given up her dream of becoming an idol. With her super powerful voice, she really deserves all the recognition.
